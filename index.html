Kendo at UIUC

We are a Registered Student Organization (RSO) as well as a registered club under the Midwest Kendo Federation (MWKF).  Our regular practice times are Fridays 7-9pm and Sunday 1-3pm (Kata 12-1pm) at Multipurpose room 1 in Campus Recreation Center East (CRCE). 

Beginners are welcome! 

We have many beginners who join our club at the beginning of every semester. No prior experience in kendo or sports is required. Please wear gym clothes to come to the first practice and we will provide the rest of the equipment and instruction. 

Visiting UIUC Kendo Club

Are you a fellow kendoka interested in visiting UIUC for a godokeiko practice? We are always happy to have visiting kendokas to join our practices. We have had great godokeiko sessions in the past with several Midwest dojos, including Bloomington Kendo Club and Purdue Kendo Club.  Please contact us with a proposed date for the visit, preferably during one of our regular practice times.
